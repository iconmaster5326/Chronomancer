Some tips:
  • Shift-click a skill to spec or respec as many points as poissible to or from it.
  • Right-click something to swap it out with something else.
  • Shift-Right-click something you chose to reset your choice. (or ctrl-right-click on Firefox.)
  • Your character is auto-saved every 30 seconds and when you close out of the window.
  • The links you get from "Get Link to Build" are not permalinks; they will not reflect changes you make after you generate the link to the build!
